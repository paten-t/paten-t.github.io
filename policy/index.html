Big Idea

  • Inventors submit their invention to a platform administered by the UN.
  • Other companies bid for the opportunity to bring that invention to the market.
  • In return for bidding, bid winners have the exclusive right to manufacture that invention.
  • This leads to competition while still providing inventors with appropriate compensation.

How it works

  • To keep this policy clear and easy to comment on, this draft of the policy is split into many pages, as opposed to one long block of text in fluent legalese.
  • Each page contains one main idea, and then several details that explore deeper.
  • Reading through the entire policy is about 15 to 30 minutes.
